ShaSol

Project Description

ShaSol is an abbreviation for SHARE SOLUTION.

ShaSol is a micro blogging digital platform that was purposely designed and developed for software engineers to share their solutions by blogging on the ShaSol platform.

Why this project

Software Engineer is an exciting career, that involves alot of research and resources to able to comeout with a workable and application. This makes an Engineer to read wide for a solutions with to a new technologies. Due to that the engineers try to have a documentation of every solution to the usage and how such technology was implemented and configured. With this platform, ShaSol, the engineer can post every solutions, links and procedures, that served as guide when engineering a solution with a software application.
